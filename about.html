About Me

My Story

Hey there! My name is Pia. I'm a creative problem solver with a passion for designing digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I'm always looking for new ways to push the boundaries of design and create something truly unique.

With a background in psychology and customer service, I bring a unique perspective to my work that helps me understand the needs & motivations of users.

I'm proficient in a variety of design tools and technologies, including Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have a strong understanding of web design principles, such as responsive design, accessibility, and usability, and I'm able to create designs tailored to the needs of specific audiences. I'm a collaborator at heart and enjoy working with developers to bring designs to life!
